Bananas Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four minions below. By clicking on a minion you will add a specific amount of banana points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total banana score to random banana number, you lose the game if your total banana score goes above the random banana number.

The banana value of each minion is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each minions.
